BEHAVIORAL ANALYSIS

How People Decide

What this experiment revealed about buyer psychology

01

Trust Before Transaction

People prefer consulting a real person they trust over messaging an official admin. Peer credibility > Brand authority for Gen Z.

02

Personal Branding > Hard Selling

Content that shows a real journey outperforms promotional ads. Authenticity drives action.

03

Storytelling Triggers Identification

"That's me" moments in content create stronger pull than feature-based marketing.

04

Decision Needs a Guide

Most prospects need someone to walk them through the decision — not just a price list.
